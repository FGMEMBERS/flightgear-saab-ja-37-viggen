Note

Various stuff adapted from other aircrafts:

F16 - Canopy. Inertia. Afterburner flame and engine glow. Texture for Strake-vortex. Cannon. Chronometer. ADI. Pedals, arrester hook.
J35F - 8 instruments and electrical system
Mig-15 - Crash code
Buccaneer - Flight direction indicator in HUD
TU-154B - Remove delay when switching to external view
Mig29 - Nav. light glow
F-14b (yasim) - Trans-mach vapor cone, guided missiles
747-400 - Reverse thrust
BAC-TSR2-GR3 - G-force breathing sounds
Cessna-208-Caravan - Radiostack
Zero - Performance monitor
F15C - Stick
Alphajet - Adapted aerobatic smoke
Crusader - Carrier catapult mechanism
pa24-250 - Glare texture for landing lights
F-14b (jsb) - Pitch and yaw dampers. Hydraulics.
l39 - Strobe light texture.

Might have forgotten some.
